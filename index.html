About Me

Hi! I am Mingda Wan(万明达), a fourth-year undergraduate student at Anhui University.

Previously, I was fortunate to be advised by Zhao Song at the UC Berkeley, Yingyu Liang and Zhenmei Shi at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, for machine learning theory and generative models.

Currently, I am looking for further research opportunities and a MPhil or PhD position in 2025 Fall. If you believe I align with your research interests, please feel free to reach out via email.
